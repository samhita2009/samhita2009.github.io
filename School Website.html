P.S.B.B Learning Leadership Academy

    

P.S.B.B.L.L.A school is located in Laxmipura village in bangalore.It spreads over an area of 10.5acres.There are seperate blocks for Primary children and Secondary children. The blocks are named as Primary Block and Senior Block. The school is ther for more than 2 decades. The school is run by effecient management and has well qualified staff members. The school is affilated to CBSE and ISO certified.

Library

There are two Libraries, one for the primary block and one for the senior block.There are more than 10,000 subject and story books across various journals. The library in the school is a store house of knowledge. Children from class IV and above can borrow library books to read at home.

Science Laboratory

There are seperate Physics, Chemistry and Biology Laboratories to perform experiments in each subject from Class VII to XII. The Laboratories have advanced equipments and facilities to conduct experiments.

Computer Laboratory

There is a Robotics Laboratory and Computer Laboratories. The labs have all together 200 laptops with Windows Operating System and advanced softwares for children to work and have hands-on experiments.

Playground

The school has a big playground where children play various sports. The school sports day is conductedin the playground every year. Various activities like yoga also happen in the playground. Morning assemblies are conducted in the school amphi theatre and playground.
